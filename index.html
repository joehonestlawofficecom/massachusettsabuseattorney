Compassionate Legal Advocacy for Survivors

Honest Law – Massachusetts Abuse Attorneys Who Listen and Act

Honest Law provides trauma-informed legal support for survivors of abuse throughout Massachusetts. We understand the courage it takes to speak up, and we’re here to ensure your voice is heard, your story is respected, and your rights are protected.

Types of Abuse Cases We Handle

  • Sexual abuse and assault

  • Domestic violence and intimate partner abuse

  • Child abuse and neglect

  • Institutional abuse (schools, religious organizations, care facilities)

Our Approach

  • Trauma-informed representation that prioritizes your safety and dignity

  • Confidential consultations with zero pressure or judgment

  • Experienced attorneys who understand the legal and emotional complexities

  • Statewide support—wherever you are in Massachusetts, we’re here

We believe survivors. We fight for justice. And we walk beside you every step of the way. If you’re ready to take action—or just want to explore your options—reach out to Honest Law today.

Attorney Daniel Faneuf

Daniel Faneuf has earned national recognition as the founder of Faneuf Law Group. His impact on personal injury law has been highlighted by The American Bar Association, Mass Lawyers Weekly, The Boston Globe, Telemundo, and National Public Radio. He has secured multi-million-dollar verdicts and settlements for injury victims. His professional distinctions include Super Lawyer honors and AVVO recognition. He supports community and veterans initiatives through the Robert F. Kennedy, Sr. Community Alliance and the First New Hampshire Cavalry Veterans Association.
